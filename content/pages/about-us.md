## Six Core Values (all verbatim)

### 1. High Standards
We maintain a standard of excellence in personalized dental care, allowing us to deliver the quality services our patients deserve. Through comprehensive treatment planning and the use of restorative and cosmetic dentistry, we help you achieve optimal dental health. In the event of an emergency, we prioritize seeing and caring for you promptly.

### 2. Education & Prevention
We believe that preventive care and education are key to optimal dental health. Our focus is on providing comprehensive 'dental health care' rather than just 'disease care.' This includes thorough exams, oral cancer screenings, and necessary x-rays, alongside routine cleanings, flossing, sealants, and fluoride treatments to prevent dental issues. We prioritize both the beauty of your smile and your overall health by reviewing your medical history to stay informed of any factors that might impact your dental care.

### 3. A Positive Experience
We build trust by treating each patient as a unique individual. Understanding that dental visits can be anxiety-inducing, our team is committed to providing a relaxing and positive experience with personalized care. Thank you for choosing us for your dental needs; we look forward to serving you.

### 4. Technology
Dr. Martenson stays up-to-date with the latest advances in cosmetic dental science, utilizing modern tools and techniques to enhance restorative and cosmetic care. Our office employs state-of-the-art technology, including digital radiography, which reduces radiation exposure by 80-90% and allows for instant, detailed imaging. These digital x-rays are essential for detecting hidden dental issues and ensuring accurate treatment planning.

### 5. Training & Expertise
Our highly trained team is dedicated to maintaining your dental health through continuous education. We stay current with the latest techniques, products, and equipment by attending industry events and being active members of professional dental associations.

### 6. Uncompromising Safety
We prioritize infection control by adhering to strict sterilization and cross-contamination protocols based on guidelines from the ADA, OSHA, and CDC to protect both our patients and staff.
