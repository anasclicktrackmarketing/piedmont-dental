# Dental Procedures

## Four Primary Service Cards (verbatim)

### 1. Dental Cleanings & Prevention
Piedmont Dental by Design provides professional cleanings to remove plaque and tartar while promoting proper home care to prevent cavities and gum disease.
**Link:** Read More → `cleanings-prevention/`

### 2. Cosmetic Dentistry
Piedmont Dental by Design offers cosmetic dentistry services like teeth whitening, veneers, and bonding to enhance smiles. Our treatments improve the appearance of teeth while maintaining natural function and health.
**Link:** Read More → `patient-resources/cosmetic-dentistry/`

### 3. Periodontal Services
Includes deep cleanings and gum disease treatments, to maintain healthy gums and prevent tooth loss. We offer procedures like scaling and root planing to remove harmful bacteria and restore gum health.
**Link:** Read More → `procedures/periodontal-services/`

### 4. Dental Restorations
Piedmont Dental by Design offers dental restorations like fillings, crowns, and implants to repair damaged or missing teeth. Our treatments restore function, strength, and aesthetics for a healthy, natural smile.
**Link:** Read More → `patient-resources/restorations/`
