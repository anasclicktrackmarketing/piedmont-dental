## Transforming Smiles with Composite & Porcelain Veneers

Veneers can be used for one tooth or all of your most visible teeth to help complete your smile. Dr. Martenson can address everything from dental chips and cracks to slightly crooked, worn, or oddly-shapes teeth with veneers.

## Professional Teeth Whitening for a Brighter, Whiter Smile

Tooth enamel is strong but porous. This means that dark foods and drinks can easily stain your teeth. These foods and beverages have intense color pigments that, when enamel is exposed to them, cause discoloration.

Cosmetic teeth whitening uses powerful solutions formulated to safely and effectively penetrate the enamel and break up stains within your smile. At the dental practice of Jill Martenson, DDS, we provide several options to brighten your smile with in-office and take-home treatments. Our team uses trusted brands such as Philips Zoom and Colgate Optic White!

## Comfortable In-Office Teeth Whitening

Eliminating surface stains at our office delivers transformative results just in time for a wedding, family portraits, work events, and other social gatherings!

In-office whitening works to eliminate years of surface stains, often in as little as an hour depending on your level of discoloration. ## Invisalign® Aligners for Improving Smiles Discreetly

Dr. Martenson and her team provide a comprehensive way to straighten your teeth and improve dental health with Invisalign. Invisalign is a popular alternative to traditional metal braces. This orthodontic system includes a series of clear plastic trays, making treatment virtually invisible. Unlike braces, Invisalign aligners are removable, requiring no changes to your diet, lifestyle, or oral hygiene routine.

Invisalign trays must be worn for at least 22 hours a day to ensure the best possible results. Complying with treatment guidelines helps to reduce the risk of orthodontic relapse and keeps your progress on the right track. This is why we solely recommend Invisalign to individuals eighteen years old and up.

## 7 Orthodontic Issues Invisalign Can Correct

Invisalign is effective at treating mild to moderate orthodontic issues, such as:

- Crooked Teeth
- Dental Crowding
- Gaps between Teeth
- Overbites
- Underbites
- Crossbites
- Open bites

## Cosmetic Periodontal Care

Sometimes, achieving a radiant and lasting smile requires care focused on your gums. Having a periodontist come in to our office allows us to provide specialized crown lengthening procedures and gum disease treatment.

Crown lengthening is a cosmetic surgery procedure designed to remove excess gum tissue that makes teeth appear shorter. The periodontist uses advanced technology and innovative tools to remove and reshape gum tissue, resulting in a more balanced smile.

## Digital Smile Design for Creating Your Dream Smile

Perhaps one of the most advanced cosmetic solutions we offer is Digital Smile Design. We use this innovative method to help create and visualize your perfect smile before performing a single procedure!

The Digital Smile Design process involves advanced technology. Often, our cosmetic dentist will take 3D images and videos to capture every angle of your smile. We also use digital intraoral scanners to obtain this essential information and import the data into our Digital Smile Design software.

These innovative tools are extremely helpful for providing 360 degrees views of the mouth and projecting the pictures on a screen in real-time. This ensures all the necessary angles of your smile are captured to create the most accurate visual representation of your final treatment results.

Additionally, Dr. Martenson carefully evaluates everything from the current shade and shape of your teeth to your gums, facial features, skin tone, and how your lips move when you speak. From there, we work with you to design a strategic treatment plan exclusively designed to address issues, enhance what you love about your smile, and improve what you don't.

## Cosmetic Dentistry FAQs

**Q1: What is the best cosmetic dental procedure for a brighter smile?**

A1: Teeth whitening is the fastest and most affordable option to enhance brightness, but veneers or bonding can address additional imperfections.

**Q2: How long do veneers last?**

A2: Porcelain veneers typically last 10–15 years with proper care and regular dental visits.

**Q3: Is Invisalign® as effective as traditional braces?**

A3: Invisalign® is highly effective for mild to moderate alignment issues, offering discreet and comfortable treatment that you can do over time at home.

**Q4: Can cosmetic dentistry improve dental function?**

A4: Yes. Many cosmetic procedures not only enhance appearance but also improve bite, comfort, and oral health.
