# Streamline Your Visit with Easy Patient Forms | Piedmont Dental

## Patient Forms

If you are an existing patient who needs to update your information, please click on the "PATIENT FORMS" tab below and update your information.

*No need to print out the forms as they will automatically upload into our system.

*If you are a new patient who has never been into our office before, we ask that you fill out your forms online prior to your appointment. Filling them out online will allow us to attend to your medical needs more quickly than completing them on your arrival.

* If you cannot fill out your forms online, please arrive 10 minutes prior to your appointment, and we will have you fill them out in office on our iPAD.

Thank you and please call our office at **(510) 350-3937** if you have any questions at all.
