# Understanding Dental Insurance Options Today | Piedmont Dental

## Insurance

The practice accepts most dental insurance programs, including non-managed care, indemnity (traditional) and PPO out-of-network plans. They are not part of any managed care network.

Key points:

- "our accounting staff will prepare all the necessary forms for your dental benefits"
- Patients are responsible for their total obligation if insurance coverage is less than anticipated
- Staff will submit pre-treatment estimates to insurance companies
- Fees charged to insured patients match those charged to all patients for similar services
- Insurance allowances may be based on fixed fee schedules that differ from the practice's usual fees
- "different insurance companies vary greatly in the types of coverage available"
- Claims processing times vary significantly among providers
