## Porcelain Crowns (Caps)

The page showcases porcelain crown (cap) restorations through multiple before-and-after case studies:

**Case 1:** Front central tooth with poor restoration replaced with an all-porcelain crown, now properly contoured to match the gumline and appearing natural.

**Case 2:** Upper arch rehabilitation featuring 8 upper all-porcelain crowns addressing stained teeth, worn incisal edges, and gum recession. Result shows uniform gumline and enhanced smile.

**Case 3:** Single tooth replacement of upper central incisor using implant with all-porcelain crown for restored smile and function.

**Case 4:** Upper and lower arch rehabilitation using implant-supported upper denture and lower implants with crowns.
