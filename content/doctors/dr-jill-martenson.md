# Meet Dr. Jill Martenson | 27 Years of Expert Dental Care

**Dr Jill Martenson**

Dr. Jill Martenson is a highly accomplished dentist with a passion for providing exceptional dental care. She has a strong educational background and a deep commitment to her profession, making her a trusted name in the field of dentistry.

## Badges / Callouts

- Lead dentist
- 29 Years Of Experience
- Best Esthetic Dentist 2024
- The 2013 Angie's List Super Service Award (SSA)
- owner of Piedmont Dental by Design

## Education

1991: Dr. Martenson graduated with honors from UCLA with a BS degree in Kinesiology.

1996: She graduated with high honors from UOP School of Dentistry.

Post-Graduation: Completed a General Practice Residency, an advanced dental educational program, at the University of Colorado Health Science Center in Denver.

## Career Progression

1997: Dr. Martenson returned to the Bay Area and joined Piedmont Dental by Design as an associate dentist.

2000: Became a partner with Dr. Herrero at Piedmont Dental by Design.

2014: After Dr. Herrero's retirement, Dr. Martenson acquired the practice, continuing to provide high-quality dental care.

## Professional Involvement

Dr. Martenson is actively involved in various national and local dental associations, including:

- American Dental Association (ADA)
- California Dental Association (CDA)
- Alameda County Dental Society
- American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry
- New Dentists Alliance
- Academy of General Dentistry
- International Congress of Implantology

## Specialization

- Dr. Martenson has been a member of an Implant Study Club since 1997, incorporating implant treatment planning and restoration into her practice.
- She is dedicated to continuing education, consistently adding hours to her CE courses in areas such as implants, aesthetics, and bonding.
- Additionally, she takes courses in personal growth and leadership to further enhance her practice.

## Personal Life

### Family and Interests

- Dr. Martenson is married to Mike McDonald, and they have two daughters, Jenner and Erin.
- The family enjoys exploring the East Bay's parks, lakes, and local farmer's markets.
- They also love skiing trips to Lake Tahoe, beach outings to Santa Cruz, and traveling across the country to visit family.
