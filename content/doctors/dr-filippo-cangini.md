# Experienced Board-Certified Periodontist | Dr. Filippo Cangini

**Dr Filippo Cangini**

**Board-Certified Periodontist**

**25 Years Of Experience**

Dr. Filippo Cangini is a board-certified periodontist. He graduated from the University of Siena in 1994, earned a Master of Oral Sciences from the University of Buffalo in 2000, and completed specialized training in periodontics and implant dentistry at the University of Rochester in 2011.

## Education

Dental School: Graduated from the University of Siena, Italy, in 1994.

## Advanced Training

General Dentistry: Completed an Advanced Education in General Dentistry at the University of Rochester, Eastman Dental Center, New York, in December 2006.

Specialized Training: Received a Certificate in Periodontics and Implant Dentistry from the University of Rochester, Eastman Dental Center in June 2011.

## Professional Credentials

Board Certification: Dr. Cangini is a board-certified periodontist, bringing extensive experience and passion to his field.
