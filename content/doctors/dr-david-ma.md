# Personalized Dental Care with Dr. David Ma | Piedmont Dental

**Dr David Ma**

**Accepting New Patients!**

**4 Years Of Experience**

**Early Life:** Born in Japan but grew up in the Bay Area, which he considers home.

**Undergraduate Degree:** Earned degree in Neurobiology, Physiology, and Behavior from UC Davis.

**Dental Degree:** Graduated with honors in clinical practice and community service from the University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine.

## Education

Dr. Ma earned his degree in Neurobiology, Physiology, and Behavior from the University of California, Davis (UC Davis).

He received his dental degree from the University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine, where he graduated with honors in clinical practice and community service.

After completing dental school, Dr. Ma pursued further training by completing a General Practice Residency at the Northern California Veteran Affairs Hospital. During this residency, he gained extensive experience in full mouth rehabilitations and complex treatment planning.

## Personal Interests

Dr. Ma lives in Rockridge with his partner.

He enjoys improving his golf and tennis skills, playing with his Australian Shepherd, Albie, and cheering on Philadelphia sports teams.

## Specialization

**Full Mouth Rehabilitations:** Expert in comprehensive treatments designed to restore the function and aesthetics of the entire mouth, addressing complex dental issues with tailored solutions.

**Complex Treatment Planning:** Specializes in creating detailed and personalized treatment plans for patients, focusing on proactive and effective strategies to manage and improve their dental health.

**Cosmetic Dentistry:** Offers a range of cosmetic procedures aimed at enhancing the appearance of patients' smiles, including teeth whitening, veneers, and other aesthetic treatments.

**Preventive and Restorative Dentistry:** Expertise includes preventive care and restorative treatments such as fillings, crowns, and bridges, designed to repair damage and maintain optimal oral health.
