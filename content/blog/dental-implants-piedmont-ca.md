# Dental Implants in Piedmont, CA: The Permanent Solution for Missing Teeth

**Author:** Published by Piedmont Dental by Design
**Category tag:** Restorative Dentistry

## Opening (verbatim)

Losing a tooth — whether from decay, gum disease, trauma, or extraction — is more than a cosmetic inconvenience. It is a structural and functional issue that, if left unaddressed, can have serious consequences for your oral health over time. Adjacent teeth drift into the gap, changing your bite. Jawbone beneath the empty socket begins to shrink through a process called resorption. The surrounding gum tissue recedes. Speaking and chewing become more difficult.

Dental implants are widely recognized by dental professionals as the gold standard solution for missing teeth — and for good reason. They are the only tooth replacement option that replaces the entire tooth structure, including the root, for a result that looks, feels, and functions like a natural tooth. At Piedmont Dental by Design, Dr. Jill Martenson provides comprehensive dental implant care to patients throughout Piedmont, Oakland, Berkeley, and the greater East Bay, combining clinical expertise with compassionate, patient-centered care.
