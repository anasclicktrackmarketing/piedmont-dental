# Why Piedmont Families Trust a General Dentist for Complete Oral Health

## Opening (verbatim)

Choosing a dentist for your entire household is one of the most significant health decisions a family can make. From a child's very first dental visit to a grandparent's routine cleaning, a trusted general dentist builds the foundation for lifelong oral wellness.

The practice has served families in Piedmont, Oakland, Berkeley, Rockridge, Montclair, and the greater East Bay for 30 years with individualized care. General dentistry encompasses far more than basic procedures — it represents a comprehensive, ongoing relationship between a dentist and a patient that monitors oral health over time and prevents escalating issues.
