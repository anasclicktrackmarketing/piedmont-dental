## Comprehensive Dental Services in Oakland

### Preventive Dentistry

The practice emphasizes preventive care through:

- Regular dental cleanings and examinations to prevent cavities and gum disease
- Advanced digital X-rays with minimal radiation exposure
- Fluoride treatments to strengthen tooth enamel
- Dental sealants for cavity protection
- Patient education on proper home oral care techniques

### Cosmetic Dentistry

Services designed to enhance smile appearance include:

- Professional teeth whitening treatments
- Custom porcelain veneers for chips, gaps, and discoloration
- Invisalign clear aligners as a discreet alternative to traditional braces
- Comprehensive smile design planning

Dr. Martenson employs modern techniques to create natural-looking results that complement facial features.

### Restorative Dentistry

Restorative services address function and integrity:

- Tooth-colored composite fillings
- Crowns and bridges for damaged or missing teeth
- Dental implants for permanent tooth replacement
- Complete and partial dentures

The practice utilizes advanced technology to ensure efficient, comfortable, and personalized restorative procedures.
