# What Is Invisalign? | Piedmont Dental by Design

## Straighten Your Smile with Invisalign at Piedmont Dental by Design

## What Is Invisalign?

Invisalign is a modern orthodontic treatment that straightens teeth using a series of clear, removable aligners. The aligners are nearly invisible and address crowding, gaps, overbites, and underbites without traditional braces' discomfort or appearance.

The treatment uses aligners made from "SmartTrack®," a patented thermoplastic material.

### Treatment Process at Piedmont Dental by Design

- Initial Invisalign Consultation: Evaluation for candidacy
- 3D Digital Imaging & Treatment Plan: Advanced scanning creates personalized plan
- Custom Clear Aligners: Series of custom aligners worn 1–2 weeks each
- Progress Checkups: Visits every 6–8 weeks
- Retainers After Invisalign: Post-treatment retention

## Invisalign vs. Traditional Braces

| Feature | Invisalign | Traditional Braces |
|---------|-----------|-------------------|
| Appearance | Clear and nearly invisible | Visible metal brackets |
| Comfort | Smooth plastic aligners | Metal wires can cause irritation |
| Removability | Removable for meals and brushing | Fixed and non-removable |
| Maintenance | Easy to clean | More difficult oral hygiene |
| Food Restrictions | Minimal | Many food limitations |
| Office Visits | Every 6–8 weeks | Usually monthly |

## Who Is a Good Candidate for Invisalign?

Ideal candidates have:

- Mild to moderate crowding
- Gaps between teeth
- Overbites and underbites
- Crossbites
- Relapsed orthodontic treatments

## Benefits of Invisalign Treatment

- Aesthetic Appeal: Clear and discreet for adults and teens
- Comfort: Smooth plastic reduces irritation
- Removability: Easy eating, brushing, and flossing
- Fewer Dental Visits: No wires or brackets to adjust
- Predictable Results: Preview new smile via 3D imaging

## How Long Does Invisalign Take To Work?

Most treatments take between 6–18 months depending on complexity. Optimal results require wearing aligners "20 to 22 hours per day."

## How Much Does Invisalign Cost?

Invisalign typically costs between "$3,000 and $7,000," depending on case complexity and location. Payment plans are available.

## Maintaining Your Smile After Invisalign

Retainers, typically worn at night, prevent tooth shifting and maintain results.

## Frequently Asked Questions About Invisalign

**Q: Is Invisalign painful?**
A: "Some mild discomfort is normal with each new aligner, but it's generally far less than with metal braces."

**Q: Can I eat with Invisalign?**
A: "No. Always remove your aligners before eating or drinking anything other than water."

**Q: How do I clean my Invisalign aligners?**
A: "Clean them daily with warm water, a soft toothbrush, or Invisalign cleaning crystals."

**Q: What if I lose an aligner?**
A: Contact the practice immediately for guidance on replacement.
